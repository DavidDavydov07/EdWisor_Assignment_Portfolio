About

I'm David Davydov, an aspiring MEAN Stack Developer and hobbyist modder/programmer. I grew up using my mother's computer. With the entire Adobe Suite at my fingertips I started learning how to use tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and Flash. I found out soon after discovering the Adobe suite that I could create small apps and games in Flash and started to learn programming via Action Script 2.0/3.0. Between aspiring to be like my mother and my own desire to create interesting content for others to explore I've ended up becoming a jack of all trades, learning how to model/rig, draw, manipulate images, animate, script, etc; learning whatever I need to learn in order to create what I've envisioned.

Although I'm a only intermediately skilled with all the tools I've learned throughout the years the result of it all is being a fast-learning easy-to-adapt individual who can learn almost any technology or technique effortlessly.

Recently I've decided to devote my time towards becoming a MEAN Stack Developer because being able to work on the front end, back end, and CDN reminds me of modding, needing multiple skills and knowing how to put them together in order to create my clients vision. It makes projects more personal and immersive, and gives me enough variety to keep things fresh throughout my career.
